Dedication

I am committed to various causes related to civil engineering and youth development

I believe that everyone should have a fair chance at life. Because of this I have been part of the organisation of multiple charity events hosted at my hometown and school. Furthermore I have attended multiple protests against animal cruelty and domestic abuse. I strongly believe that my country's youth will bring order and change.
